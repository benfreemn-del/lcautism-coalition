Important Update

Postponed to November 2026

The Lewis County Autism Coalition has made the strategic decision to postpone the 15th Annual Southwest Washington Autism Conference to 2026. This was not an easy decision, but it reflects the very real demands of launching our first-ever physical space, the Spectrum & Development Community Center, while also juggling new contracts, expanded programming, and the broader operational responsibilities that come with growth.

The board agreed that protecting quality, mission alignment, and long-term sustainability is ultimately the best way to serve our community, even when that means making tough calls. Please know this is an intentional recalibration, with every intention of returning stronger in 2026. In the meantime, we remain deeply committed to meaningful engagement.
