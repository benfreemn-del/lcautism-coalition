WA INCLUDE Partnership

Statewide Collaboration

Working together across Washington State to improve autism care, bridge gaps in rural communities, and build a coordinated system of support for every family.

The Initiative

What Is WA INCLUDE?

WA INCLUDE is a statewide initiative focused on improving autism care across Washington. It uses the ECHO (Extension for Community Healthcare Outcomes) model to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and community organizations, ensuring that families everywhere have access to high-quality, coordinated support.

By bringing together clinicians, educators, advocates, and families, WA INCLUDE creates a shared learning environment that strengthens the capacity of communities to serve autistic and neurodiverse individuals effectively.

Our Contribution

LCAC's Role in WA INCLUDE

As an active partner in the WA INCLUDE initiative, the Lewis County Autism Coalition brings a critical rural perspective to statewide efforts.

ECHO Training Participation

We actively participate in ECHO training sessions, learning from specialists and sharing our on-the-ground experiences to improve care delivery in rural settings.

Rural Perspective

We bring the unique challenges and strengths of rural Lewis County to the conversation, helping shape statewide policies and practices that work for communities like ours.

Best Practices Implementation

We support the implementation of evidence-based best practices locally, ensuring our families benefit from the latest advancements in autism care and support.

Connecting Families to Care

We help connect local families to coordinated, comprehensive care by serving as a bridge between statewide resources and community-level needs.

Making a Difference

Local Impact

Through our WA INCLUDE partnership, we are strengthening the local autism care ecosystem in Lewis County. Our SMART Team integrates seamlessly with statewide initiatives, and our co-location with a pediatric Center of Excellence provides families with a streamlined path from concern to support.

✓ SMART Team integration with statewide coordination
✓ Co-location with pediatric Center of Excellence
✓ Peer navigation and family support collaboration
✓ Bridging gaps for rural families facing delays and fragmented services

Closing the Gap for Rural Families

Rural families often face significant delays in autism diagnosis and encounter fragmented, hard-to-access services. Through our statewide partnerships, LCAC is working to close these gaps and ensure every family in Lewis County has timely access to the support they deserve.
