Halloween for Everyone

Teal Pumpkin Trunk-or-Treat Sponsor

Help us host a sensory-friendly, allergen-aware Halloween event where every child in Lewis County — every ability, every dietary need — can trick-or-treat in safety and joy.

Why Teal?

A Pumpkin That Says "Welcome"

A teal pumpkin on the porch means non-food treats are available — toys, stickers, glow sticks. It's a small but powerful signal that says, "Your child is welcome here, no matter their allergies or sensory needs."
